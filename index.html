Guillem Hurault

PhD researcher

Imperial College London

Biography

I am a research assistant and PhD researcher in the Biological Control Systems Lab, under the supervision of Dr Reiko Tanaka, in the Department of Bioengineering of Imperial College London.

My research is on the development of statistical machine learning models to predict and control eczema, with a particular interest in Bayesian statistics and time-series forecasting.

  • Bayesian modelling
